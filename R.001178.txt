Our best attempt to find what should be on the shelf.  Enhanced to allow you to select Branch and Collection Code and Shelving Location.

----------

SELECT items.onloan,items.itemcallnumber,biblio.title,items.barcode FROM items JOIN biblio USING (biblionumber) WHERE items.homebranch=<<Pick your branch|branches>> AND items.ccode=<<Pick your collection code|CCODE>> AND items.location=<<location|LOC>> AND items.damaged=0 AND items.itemlost=0 AND items.notforloan=0 ORDER BY items.onloan, items.itemcallnumber ASC
