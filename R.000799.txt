Enhanced - Pick your Branch, run and get a list of items checked out at your branch that belonged to another library.  Useful for Collection DEVELOPMENT.   

----------

SELECT statistics.branch as Library, biblio.title, biblio.author, items.itemcallnumber, items.itype, items.ccode, items.location, items.homebranch as ItemBranch, statistics.itemtype FROM statistics  LEFT JOIN items USING (itemnumber) LEFT JOIN biblio USING(biblionumber) WHERE statistics.branch != items.homebranch AND statistics.type IN ('issue', 'renew') AND MONTH(statistics.datetime)= <<Month>> and YEAR(statistics.datetime)=<<Year>> and statistics.branch = <<Pick your branch|branches>> ORDER BY statistics.branch, biblio.title
