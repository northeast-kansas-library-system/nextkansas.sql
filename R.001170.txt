Opposite of transfers to receive report, only lists items that were sent more than 7 days ago; enhanced, run as is. #transfers

<p><span style="background-color: darkred; color: white">This report is scheduled for deletion on July 1, 2018.</p>

----------

select bt.frombranch as 'Sending Library', i.homebranch as 'Owning Library', bt.tobranch as 'receiving library', bt.datesent as 'Date Sent', CONCAT('<a href="/cgi-bin/koha/catalogue/detail.pl?biblionumber=',i.biblionumber,'" target="_blank">',b.title,'</a>') as title, i.barcode, i.itemcallnumber, i.ccode from branchtransfers bt join items i USING(itemnumber) join biblio b USING(biblionumber) where bt.frombranch = <<branch|branches>> and bt.datearrived is NULL AND bt.datesent < DATE_ADD(CURDATE(),INTERVAL -7 DAY) ORDER BY bt.datesent ASC
