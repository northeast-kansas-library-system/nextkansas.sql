This report allows you to limit to your library, to enter a zipcode and then the report provides a list of patrons not in that zipcode.

----------

SELECT firstname, surname, address, city, zipcode, email, phone, categorycode, dateenrolled, dateexpiry FROM borrowers WHERE branchcode=<<branch|branches>> AND zipcode <> <<enter zipcode>> ORDER BY surname
