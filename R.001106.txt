This report will let you find out what items (and to whom) are checked out for a given library in a given zip code.

----------

select borrowers.firstname, borrowers.surname, borrowers.address, biblio.title, items.barcode, issues.date_due from issues join borrowers USING(borrowernumber) join items using(itemnumber) join biblio using(biblionumber)  where  borrowers.zipcode = <<enter zip code>> and borrowers.branchcode =<<Select your Library|branches>>
