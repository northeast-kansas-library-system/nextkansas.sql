A list of the messages sent to a particular patron. Enter borrowernumber in the parameter box (patrons who have email addresses, anyway). 

----------

SELECT message_id, CONCAT('<a href=\"/cgi-bin/koha/circ/circulation.pl?borrowernumber=',borrowernumber,'\" target="_blank">',borrowernumber,'</a>'), borrowernumber, subject, content, metadata, letter_code, message_transport_type, status, time_queued, to_address, from_address, content_type FROM message_queue WHERE borrowernumber=<<enter borrower number>> GROUP BY message_id ORDER BY time_queued DESC
